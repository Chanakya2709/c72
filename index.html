The Best ever bookshop in the world
In 1893, Cole’s Book Arcade in Melbourne is the grandest bookshop in the world, brimming with every curiosity imaginable. Each day brings fresh delights for the siblings: voice-changing sweets, talking parrots, a new story written just for them by their eccentric father. Pearl and Vally Cole live in a bookshop.

Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Review ⭐ ‘Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows’ is a very satisfying ending to the long-loved book series about the boy wizard Harry Potter. It does justice to the reputation of the entire series by telling the story of the finale with great finesse.
